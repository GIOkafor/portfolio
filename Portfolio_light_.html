RevMuzik

Live Link

Product Overview

RevMuzik is a platform that makes it possible for owners of venues - such as bars, restaurants, lounges etc - to find artists for live music performances. These performances are then showcased to music lovers looking for a good time out.

Key Points

The tech stack was Angular on the frontend(HTML, SCSS & JS), Java on the backend and a combination of AWS & GCP for filling in the gaps in between.

During my time there, I worked primarily as a front end engineer, working closely with the backend team, making use of whatever endpoints they provisioned.

Here are a few highlights of some features I personally worked on

Gig Search & discovery for unauthenticated users

Part of our user acquisition strategy involved giving prospective users a taste of what to expect when they join the site.

Technical challenge was to give them something to see, yet persist their search so they could easily pick up from where they left off, once they’d created an account.

User Authentication

User authentication is handled using firebase authentication, earlier on my team experimented with AWS Cognito for user authentication but had to drop it and switch to firebase due to some limitations.

Chats & Proposals

For handling chats, i rely on data coming from the firebase realtime datastore(a non relational db), which I handle using observable streams and rxjs
